SIMPLE BANANA BREAD

The best thing that can happen to bananas. No joke, this is the best banana bread you will ever eat. It's absolutely foolproof, soft, moist, sweel... you'll love it!

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking time: 60 minutes
  • Serves: 3 servings
Ingredients
Quantity
Ripe bananas 3 large
Oil 1/4 cup(60ml)
Plant milk 1/4 cup(60ml)
Sugar 1/3 cup(65g)
Flour 2 cups(250g)
Cocoa powder 1 tbsp
Water 6 tbsp

Instructions:
Step 1:Mash banana.
Step 2:Combine wet and dry ingredients in deprecate bowls then stir the dry in the wet
Step 3: Bake at 175C(350F) for 50-60 minutes
Step 4: Combine all the ingredients for spread.
Step 5: Serve!
