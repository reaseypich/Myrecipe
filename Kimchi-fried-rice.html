KIMCHI FRIED RICE

Don't be intimidated by its bright red color! Kimchi fried rice is super flavorful and actually not too spicy. It's also a very versatile recipe the use up any leftover rice and vegetables sitting in the back of your fridge. Top it with an egg and you've got yourself an easy, delicious meal!

  • Prep time: 5 minutes
  • Cooking time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 servings
Ingredients
Quantity
Cooked short grain rice 400g
Fried tofu puffs 100g
Small onion 1/2
Garlic 1 clove
Limchi 100g
Kimchi liquid 2 tbsp
Dark soy sauce 1 tbsp
Sesame oil 2 tsp
Butter 1 tbsp
Spring onion 1
Eggs (opt) 2
Sesame seeds 1 tbsp
Chopped spring onion 1 tbsp
Sheet seaweed, cut 1/2

Instructions:
Step 1:Finely mince garlic, chop spring onion, small dice onion and medium dice kimchi.
Step 2: Heat up a large nonstick skillet over medium-low heat and melt butter in it.
Step 3: Add onion and garlic, and stir fry for about 3 minutes.
Step 4: Add kimchi and stir fry for about 2 minutes.
Step 5: Add fried tofu puffs, kimchi liquid and dark soy sauce. Stir fry for about 2 minutes.
Step 6: Add rice, breaking it up using your spatula. Stir fry for about 2 minutes.
Step 7: Drizzle in sesame oil and add chopped spring onion, and stir fry for about 5 minutes until rice starts to brown lightly. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Then remove from heat and set aside.
Step 8: In a small nonstick pan, fry the eggs sunny side up.
Step 9: Transfer rice to serving bowls and top with fried eggs, seaweed strips and sesame seeds.
